Web Design Agency

Create a simple website for a web design agency

Usually with web design agencies, you'll see them demonstrating their best work, but their websites are usually quite simple.

So something with a big block of text telling people what they do & then just simple clean design.

This is what we're going to be creating.

We're going to be a Creative Design Agency @ dev.com & we've got 2 little blocks of text that describes a little bit about our work, & finally, @ the bottom, we've got a footer with our company motto, "Create. Develop. Design."

But more importantly, our website actually scales responsively.

So when we create our website, we're going to make sure that when it's displayed on a smaller screen, namely an iPhone, that our blocks now change & are centered & are responsive to make better use of the screen real estate.

So instead of having it side by side, when the screen size is a certain size, they actually stack on top of each other.

So this is going to require a little bit of the knowledge that you learned in the lesson about the Display property, Floats & also Responsiveness.

You can, of course, design everything completely from scratch, but I think it's important that we actually have a good pace of learning & we're practicing the things that we've actually just learned about, rather than doing everything from scratch & not focusing on the important things.

Using what you've learned in this section, you should be perfectly able to add the modifications to the index.html to create the look & feel.

Remember, I want you to try out putting in responsiveness & try out some of the things you learned in this section, such as block, inline-block & floats.

Just put some of those things into the CSS so that you have some practice doing these things.
